Here are some instructions on how to use Auto-Schedule:


1) Log onto WebReg. WebReg can be accesed by going to https://students.ucsd.edu/my-tritonlink/tools/, scrolling down and clicking on the WebReg link underneath the header "Classes & enrollment tools"

2) Use your Student ID/username and your UCSD password to login to WebReg. Make sure that you authenticate with Duo.

3) Click the drop-down menu, select the quarter that your schedule is in, and click go.

4) Take a screenshot of WebReg so that it only shows the blue boxes which displays the schedule

5) Now go back to the home page of this site. Upload the screenshot onto the site, and submit the form.

6) You may get a notification from Google saying that this application may not be safe. In this case, click "Advanced" and edit the settings in order to allow our application to send the data to your Google Calendar.

Congratulations! Your schedule has successfully been loaded onto Google Calendar. Check it out!
